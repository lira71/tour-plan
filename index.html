Mike Hamilton

Stayed 10 Nov, 2019

This is an exceptional hotel in Kyrenia. It is clean, organized and very well managed. I never stayed in the hotel but I frequently visit its restaurants and beach. The Tirkish restaurant offers delicious food including Kebabs and seafood. The beach is sandy and clean. The cabanas on the side offers a break away from the busy main beach, excellent for families with younger children. For a more secluded and private place, try the Cocobango cabanas of the Maldives cabanas. They are located on the breakwater opposite the main beach with access to the sea through a ladder. The staff are nice and friendly and the food and drinks are very generous.

Fred Nikson

Here, there are nice à la carte restaurants and night clubs for guests who do not want to use the hotel buffet. I think that the Port part should be seen, especially.
